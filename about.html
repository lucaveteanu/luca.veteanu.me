About Me

Get to know my journey in computer science and education

Who I Am

I'm a passionate high school student from Cooper City, Florida, who loves using coding to be creative and help others learn new things. I got into computer science because I was curious about how things work, and over time it’s become something I really enjoy sharing with people around me.

I like building games, coding in JavaScript, and creating virtual worlds in Minecraft, Roblox, and Fortnite. Whether I’m teaching a class, working on a project, or writing a blog post, I just like finding fun ways to make coding interesting and easy to understand.

My Philosophy

I believe that everyone has the potential to code. It's not about being naturally gifted or having a special talent. It's about curiosity, persistence, and having someone who believes in you. That's why I dedicate time to teaching others, creating learning materials, and sharing my own journey through my blog.

Through my work at local libraries and schools, I've had the privilege of introducing JavaScript and game development to dozens of students. Watching them build their first programs and seeing their excitement reminds me why I fell in love with coding in the first place.
